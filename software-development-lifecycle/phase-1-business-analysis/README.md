# Phase 1 -- Business Analysis

> All documents in this phase follow the **QRH (Quick Reference Handbook)** format unless otherwise noted. Quick references contain no explanatory prose -- only checklists, decision tables, and cross-reference links. Training materials are the exception and include educational content.

---

## Phase Overview

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Phase** | 1 -- Business Analysis |
| **Purpose** | Identify business needs, define problems, evaluate options, and authorize projects |
| **Trigger** | Business need identified (strategic, operational, regulatory, or market-driven) |
| **Outcome** | Approved Project Charter authorizing transition to Phase 2 |

---

## Entry Criteria

- [ ] Business need identified and documented
- [ ] Sponsor or business owner nominated
- [ ] Need aligns with at least one organizational strategic objective
- [ ] Initial stakeholders identified

## Exit Criteria

- [ ] Problem statement approved by sponsor
- [ ] Business case evaluated and option selected
- [ ] Risk assessment completed for selected option
- [ ] Project charter approved by governance authority
- [ ] Success criteria defined with measurable targets
- [ ] Stakeholder register established
- [ ] Phase 2 team and resources identified
